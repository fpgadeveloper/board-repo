## Configuration

Boot mode is determined by DIP switch SW16.

| Config mode       | 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 | 5 |
|-------------------|---| --| --| --|---|
| Master BPI        | x | x | 0 | 1 | 0 |
| JTAG              | x | x | 1 | 0 | 1 |

Switches 1 and 2 determine which of four possible configuration
bitstreams to load from Master BPI flash. They are connected to
A25 and A24 of the flash memory.
