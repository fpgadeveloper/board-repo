## Configuration

FPGA configuration is determined by DIP switch SW15.

| Config mode        | 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 | 5 | 6 |
|--------------------|---| --| --| --| --|---|
| JTAG               | X | X | X | X | 0 | 1 |
| JTAG SD            | X | X | X | X | 1 | 1 |
| Quad SPI           | Note 4 | Note 4 | Note 4 | Note 4 | 0 | 0 |

Notes:

1. X = don't care
2. 0 = OFF position
3. 1 = ON position
4. Switches 1 to 4 determine which of 16 bitstreams is loaded from the Quad SPI flash
