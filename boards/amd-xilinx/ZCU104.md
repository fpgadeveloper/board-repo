### Configuration

Boot mode is determined by DIP switch SW6.

| SW6               | 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 |
|-------------------|---| --| --| --|
| JTAG  (default)   | 1 | 1 | 1 | 1 |
| QSPI32            | 1 | 0 | 1 | 1 |
| SD                | 1 | 0 | 0 | 0 |
