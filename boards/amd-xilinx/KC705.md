## Notes

### Configuration

FPGA configuration is determined by DIP switch SW13.

| Config mode        | 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 | 5 |
|--------------------|---| --| --| --| --|
| JTAG               | X | X | 1 | 0 | 1 |
| Master BPI         | Note 4 | Note 4 | 0 | 1 | 0 |
| Quad SPI           | X | X | 0 | 0 | 1 |

Notes:

1. X = don't care
2. 0 = OFF position
3. 1 = ON position
4. Switches 1 and 2 are A25 and A24 respectively and determine
   which of 4 bitstreams is booted from the Linear BPI flash
