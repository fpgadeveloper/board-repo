| SW16               | 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 | 5 |

| JTAG (independent) | 1 | 0 | 0 | 0 | 0 |
| QSPI               | 0 | 0 | 0 | 1 | 0 |
| SD                 | 0 | 0 | 1 | 1 | 0 |
